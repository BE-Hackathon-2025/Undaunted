## Project Description ğŸ¤¯
**....at least 3 sentences describing what the project does and how it works**
AisleReady is a sophisticated web application designed to eliminate the guesswork of airport departure times. It intelligently synthesizes real-time data from five key sources: live traffic conditions, address location, and current and forecasted weather, official flight status, and generative AI analysis. By processing these variables, the app generates a dynamic and personalized recommendation, providing users with a precise departure time that ensures they arrive at their gate with an optimal buffer, accounting for TSA security wait times.


## Project Value ğŸ’°
**....at least 3 sentences describing your target customer and tangible benefits from your solution**
AisleReady's primary target customers are frequent business travelers and anxious leisure flyers who value punctuality and a stress-free airport experience. For these users, our solution delivers the tangible benefit of reclaimed time and reduced anxiety by providing a single, reliable departure time, eliminating the need to manually check multiple apps regarding travel. This directly translates into a smoother travel day, minimizing the risk of missed flights and the stress of unexpected delays, ensuring they start their journey with confidence.
